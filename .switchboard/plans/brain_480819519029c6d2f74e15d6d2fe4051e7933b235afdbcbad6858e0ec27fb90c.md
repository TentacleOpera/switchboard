# Relocate Kanban Automation Button and Timers to Sub-Bar

Move the automation toggle button and active column countdown timers from the right side of the main controls strip into a new compact sub-bar directly above the columns (in the negative space). Flashing status notifications will appear directly to the right of the button/timers in this sub-bar.

## Verification Plan

### Manual Verification
- Launch the Switchboard extension and navigate to the Kanban tab.
- Verify the new sub-bar containing the automation play/stop icon and the column timers is displayed directly below the main controls strip and above the board columns.
- Ensure the automation toggle works: clicking the icon toggles automation, updating the icon appearance and starting/stopping the countdown timers.
- Move a card to verify the "card advanced" flashing status message displays to the right of the timers within the sub-bar and fades out cleanly without shifting the rest of the board.
- Check that the sub-bar scales correctly on narrower views and does not cause overflow or visual clipping.
