# Implementation Plan - Toolkit Organization

This plan focuses on reorganizing how resources are presented when the app is in **Toolkit Mode**. The primary goal is to categorize the vast library by **Content Type** first.

## Proposed Changes

### Toolkit Mode (Home Screen)
*   **[MODIFY] [index.html](file:///Users/patrickvuleta/Documents/GitHub/patrickwork/designs/index.html)**
    *   Update the `refreshGoalsScreen` function for `currentMode === 'toolkit'`.
    *   **New Section: "Browse by Type"**: Large, high-fidelity tiles for **Courses**, **Deep Dives**, **Downloads**, and **Blog Posts**.
    *   **New Section: "Domain Categories"**: Below the types, maintain the ability to browse by topic (Communication, Sensory, etc.) to mirror the production app's taxonomy.

### Guides / Library Screen
*   **[MODIFY] `screen-guides`**
    *   Introduce a primary content-type switcher (Segmented Control) at the top: **All | Courses | Deep Dives | Downloads | Blogs**.
    *   Update the result cards to clearly differentiate between a "Course" (Video/Length) and a "Download" (File/Format).

## Verification Plan

### Manual Verification
1.  Switch to **Toolkit Mode** on the home screen and verify "Browse by Type" is the primary navigation method.
2.  Navigate to **Guides** and verify the content-type switcher filters the library correctly.
3.  Ensure the **Archive** tab (Diary/Graph) is unchanged and fully functional.
